HDR 影像

讓光不只被看見，也被時間帶著前進。

Light is not only seen. It moves forward with time.

HDR 影像讓天空、反射、夜色與遠方的輪廓能在同一個畫面裡共存。我希望每支影片都像一段安靜的行走，沒有過度解釋，只讓光線和場景慢慢展開。

HDR film allows skies, reflections, night tones, and distant outlines to exist together within one frame. I want each film to feel like a quiet walk, without over-explaining, letting light and place unfold at their own pace.
