HDR 攝影

安靜的風景、遠行的痕跡，以及光停留過的地方。

Quiet landscapes, traces of travel, and places where light has stayed.

我關注的不只是畫面的清晰度，而是光線如何改變一個地方的情緒。HDR 讓影像能更接近人眼在現場感受到的深度，也讓自然中的細節不必在高光與陰影之間被犧牲。

My focus is not only clarity, but how light changes the emotional temperature of a place. HDR allows an image to move closer to the depth we sense on location, preserving detail without forcing highlights and shadows to compete.
